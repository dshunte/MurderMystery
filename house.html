Dwaine is at Alex's House

I don't even know why I am here....

We went to school together almost a decade ago! So what! The Young Super Sleuth Club we called ourselves (yes I know it is corny, give us a break, we were 12!).

Ironically, I am the only one to actually go into the P.I. business.......... if you could call the trickle of cases that actually come through my door "business"; my bank account sure doesn't.

Wilma and Jane still look fantastic though, I remember I had such a crush on Jane in high school but I wasn't Mr Cool like Harry; the eternal beach bum with the Peter Pan complex, so i got friend-zoned, probably for the best, I might have ended it up stuck in a dead end job and even deader end marriage, white picket fence and the obligatory 2.5 tikes (looking at you Fred, Mr. insurance salesman).

What really would have been nice was winding up the multimillionaire dot-com startup guru like Alex. I think he really invited us over here this weekend to show off and remind Jane what she gave up, can't blame him, I would do the same thing if I was in his handmade Italian leather shoes.

I know I know, maybe all this envy is what's keeping me awake, it surely isn't this bed, i 've never felt anything so soft!

Anyway, I think I remember seeing a bottle of Brandy in the dining room that we actually didn't get into last night, maybe that will help with this insomnia.
