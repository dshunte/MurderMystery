Dwaine is in Hallway with Everyone

Abruptly awoken by my aggressive door pounding, I now have the full complement of bleary-eyed guests gathered in the hallway outside of their rooms.

I spare the more squeamish of the group the goriest of details as I struggle through the grisly task thrust upon me of being bearer of such horrendous news.

Everyone's reaction seems thoroughly genuine, without slightest trace of artifice, if not for the inescapable laws of physics and logic I would be certain that all gathered in front of me were fully innocent of this heinous crime.

But I am sure of two things; Alex was murdered by one of us and I didn't do it.

As the only trained, professional investigator and as one of Alex's oldest friends it is my duty to get to the bottom of this. With considerable effort I get everyone to agree to return to their respective rooms, not to emerge again until I have figured this out.

I think I should head to the Living Room to gather my thoughts and start my investigations.
